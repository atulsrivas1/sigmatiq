Galaxy Metaphor Explained

☀️ Sun (Core) - Yellow

The intelligence engine at the center. All data gravitates toward it for processing. Represents the AI/ML core that powers decisions.

🌍 Earth (Interface) - Blue

Where users live and interact. The outer orbit represents the human touchpoint - where data enters and decisions are executed.

🌌 Orbits - Teal

Data layers and processing stages. Information flows through these orbits, getting refined at each level as it moves between Earth and Sun.

✨ Satellites - Cream

Features, modules, and auxiliary functions that support the main system. They orbit at various levels providing specialized capabilities.
