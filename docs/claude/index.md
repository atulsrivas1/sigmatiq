## Key Features

### Trading Strategy Packs

We offer five types of trading strategies:

- **ZeroSigma** - Very fast trades (same day)
- **SwingSigma** - Hold for a few days
- **LongSigma** - Hold for months
- **OvernightSigma** - Trade overnight gaps
- **MomentumSigma** - Follow strong trends

### Safety Features

Every product includes:
- Stop losses (automatic selling to limit losses)
- Risk profiles (Conservative, Balanced, Aggressive)
- Paper trading (practice with fake money)
- Gate system (quality checks before trading)

### Dashboard Views

Track everything from one place:
- Recent models and their performance
- Live signals as they happen
- System health status
- Quick action buttons

## How It All Works Together

1. **Create** a model in Sigma Lab
2. **Test** it with historical data
3. **Practice** in Sigma Sim with fake money
