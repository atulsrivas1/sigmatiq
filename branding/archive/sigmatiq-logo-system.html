Logo Usage Guidelines

Clear Space
Maintain minimum clear space equal to the height of the "I" in SIGMATIQ around all logos.
Minimum Size
Main logo: 120px width minimum
Product logos: 100px width minimum
Icon only: 24px minimum
Color Usage
Primary: Teal gradient on dark
Secondary: Single color on light
Never use more than 2 colors in logo
Product Differentiation
Lab: Bright Teal (#00C4A7)
Sim: Deep Teal (#00D4B8)
Market: Golden (#FFB800)
Pilot: Cream (#E8E3D9)
